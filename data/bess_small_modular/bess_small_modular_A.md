# Small Modular BESS – Variant A

## Overview
A compact, modular battery energy storage unit designed for residential
and small commercial applications. The unit can be combined with additional
modules to scale system capacity.

## Key Parameters (Synthetic)
- Nominal Energy Capacity: ~120 kWh
- Nominal Power: ~60 kW
- Battery Technology: Lithium-ion (LFP)
- Round-trip Efficiency: ~90%
- Design Lifetime: 10–15 years
- Expected Cycle Life: ~5,000 cycles
- Operating Temperature Range: -10°C to +45°C

## Modularity
- Designed as a standalone module
- Multiple units can be combined into a larger system
- Typical installations range from 1 to 5 modules

## Typical Use Cases
- Residential energy storage
- Small commercial buildings
- PV self-consumption optimization
- Backup power for critical loads
