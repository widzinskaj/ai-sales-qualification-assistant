Two possible small modular BESS variants were identified based on the customer inquiry.

Variant A:
- Compact modular BESS suitable for residential and small commercial use
- Approximate capacity range: tens to low hundreds of kWh
- Approximate power range: tens of kW
- Designed for smaller installations and limited space

Variant B:
- Modular BESS designed for small business and commercial applications
- Approximate capacity range: hundreds of kWh
- Approximate power range: around 100 kW or more
- Suitable for scalable, multi-module installations
